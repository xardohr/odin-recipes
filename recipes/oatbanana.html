Oat Banana

Perfect dish for breakfast

This recipe is the perfect go to when you have a tight schedual in the morning. It is tasty, healthy and gives you energy throughout your day.

It doesn't require much time or ingredient, It's also pretty cheap. Just try it! You won't regret it

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup of milk
  • 1/2 cup uncooked Oats
  • 1 Banana
  • 1/2 tsp Cinnamon powder
