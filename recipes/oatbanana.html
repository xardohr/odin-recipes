Oat Banana


Perfect dish for breakfast

This recipe is the perfect go to when you have a tight schedual in the morning. It is tasty, healthy and gives you energy throughout your day.

It doesn't require much time or ingredient, It's also pretty cheap. Just try it! You won't regret it

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup of milk
  • 1/2 cup uncooked Oats
  • 1 Banana
  • 1/2 tsp Cinnamon powder

Steps to prepare

  1. Throw 1/2 cup of uncooked Oats inside a pot
  2. Crack the 2 eggs inside
  3. Add the milk
  4. Add Cinnamon powder
  5. Mix very well
  6. Turn on the heat to medium low
  7. Optional: You can cut the Banana to pieces and cooked it along side the Oats / or wait until it is fully cooked then cut the Banana
